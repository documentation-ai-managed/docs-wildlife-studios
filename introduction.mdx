## Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Create your free account at `https://dashboard.example.com/signup`. Verify your email to unlock full access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Perform Your First Search" icon="search">
    Enter a query like "antitrust merger guidelines" in the search bar. Refine results with jurisdiction filters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Annotate and Save" icon="save">
    Select text in a case, add notes, and save to your library. Share via secure links.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Alerts" icon="bell">
    Add cases or topics to your watchlist for email notifications on updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Navigation Basics

The interface prioritizes speed and discoverability.

- **Sidebar**: Access libraries, watchlists, and recent searches.
- **Top Bar**: Quick search, user profile, and notifications.
- **Dashboard**: Personalized feeds with trending cases and updates.

For example, advanced search syntax:

<CodeGroup tabs="Basic,Advanced">
  ```plaintext
  negligence duty of care
  ```
  ```plaintext
  "duty of care" AND negligence NOT criminal jurisdiction:US date>2020
  ```
</CodeGroup>
